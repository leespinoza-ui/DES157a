Mad Libs

Directions:
Before reading the story, write down a word for each category listed below. Don’t read the story yet! Once all the words are chosen, read the story out loud and replace each highlighted word with the word you picked.

How to Play

Mad Libs are a fun word game where you create a silly story without knowing how it will turn out. The surprise is what makes it funny!

Ask a friend to help you choose words, or challenge yourself to come up with the weirdest words you can think of.

Helpful Tips

Don’t think too hard, silly words work best!

Avoid reading ahead in the story.

Try playing more than once with different words.

Reading the final story out loud makes it even funnier.
