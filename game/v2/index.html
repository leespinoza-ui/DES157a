How to Play

  • Press Roll to roll both dice.
  • The two dice are added to your turn total.
  • Press Hold to save your turn total into your score.
  • If either die lands on 1, you lose your turn total and your turn ends.
  • The highlighted player box shows whose turn it is.
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins.
